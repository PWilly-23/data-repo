This is the Marcus theater

Covid-19 requirments: Masks are highly recommended regardless of vaccination status.

Marcus was founded by Ben Marcus in 1935. It is also a hotel company. It features the DLSX Ultra Screen theaters. It remains with Marcus as a first run theater, the Campus Cinema (near Ripon College), with titles appealing to both adults and families. It was also a restaurant franchisee, operating the Marc's Big Boy affiliate of the Big Boy Restaurants chain and serving as the Milwaukee area franchise for Kentucky Fried Chicken. Marcus sold the KFC franchises by 1992 and closed the last Marc's in 1995.
